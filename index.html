About

I am a second-year master student in the School of Engineering and Technology at CQUniversity. My main research interest centres on construction safety science and site management. My master’s research investigates the antecedents of construction workers’ safety compliance and how to improve their safety performance by developing a framework about safety compliance in the Australian construction industry.I am a student member of the Australian Institute of Quantity Surveyors (AIQS) and the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS). I hold a general construction induction card (white card), which allows me to conduct regular site visit as my research requires. I gained a bachelors’ degree in construction management and quantity surveying from Bond University, that gives me a solid foundation of construction site management, digital design and intelligent construction, site hazard identification and prevention, BIM, and construction site risk management.
